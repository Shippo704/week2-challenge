Physical Activity - Biking

This is my favourite route to take. It is scenic and long. Biking by the river means that there are fewer hills and that the air is cooler. The quantity of parks along the route means that I have many opportunities to refill my water bottle if the need arises.

My bike is a Miele Umbria hybrid road bike. It is 21-speed and has an aluminum frame. It is light and fast on smooth pavement and can handle the super tiny gravel if needed. It can go on short grass, though it is not recommended. The lack of suspension and short tires grooves mean that non-pavement surfaces are not recommended.
