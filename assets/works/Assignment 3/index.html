Matt's Summer "Vacation" Plans

People enjoy summer for various reasons:

  • Warm Weather
  • Physical Activity
  • Camping
  • Swimming
  • Sports
  • Socialising
  • Sun
  • Ice Cream
  • FREE TIME!

 

 

 

While I can get behind some of these things, I am sensitive to both heat and light. Socialising with almost anyone for more than 20 minutes will exhaust me. Summer is my least favourite season.

I disagree. I like winter better, but this assignment is supposed to be about summer, unfortunately.

Reasons I prefer winter:

  • Cold Weather
  • Snow
  • Skiing
  • Hot Chocolate and Tea
  • Warm Houses and Blankets
  • Less Socialising
  • NO BUGS!
That said, summer "vacation" can still be a time to enjoy some activities and hobbies. This website will outline the things that I will be doing this summer. I like to spend my free time biking and practising music. However, free time is hard to come by between work. As such, I haven't had a proper summer "vacation" since 2011 when I graduated from high school and worked a lot less. Previous summers I have worked two jobs, as a pharmacy assistant and as a CAO analyst at a grocery store. This summer, the work will be significantly different.
